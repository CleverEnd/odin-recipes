Grilled Radroach Meat

Description

The edible meat from mutated and irradiated cockroaches known throughout post-War America as radroaches.

Ingredients

  • Radroach Meat(1)
  • Wood(1)

Steps

  1. Hunt a Radroach.
  2. Extract the meat.
  3. Go to the nearest Cooking Station.
  4. Select Grilled Radroach from the Station Menu.
  5. Enjoy.
